Auditing Generative AI Models

Principal Investigators: Ken Holstein, Motahhare Eslami and Jason Hong

Project Mentor: Wesley Deng

Institution and Department: Carnegie Mellon University, Human-Computer Interaction Institute

Duration: May 2024 - Present

Research Focus: Auditing Generative AI Text-to-image Models

Project Responsibilities: During my time at CMU, I have deloped MIRAGE, a tool where users can compare different text-to-image AI models side by side. I have also conducted user studies to evaluate the tool's effectiveness and have presented this work at HCOMP 2024 as a Demo Paper and recently submitted to SIGCHI 2025 as a Late Breaking Work paper.

Additional Context: Although I joined this project during last Summer as part of an REU, my contract has been extended to the current academic year where I continue to conduct research with Carnegie Mellon University remotely.
